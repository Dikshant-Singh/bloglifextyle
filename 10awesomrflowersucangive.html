10 MOST ROMANTIC FLOWERS THAT YOU CAN GIVE TO GIRLS

Women just love flowers no matter of what age group they belongs to. Giving flowers always bring a smile to their face. Flowers can also help you sometimes to control their anger or top change their mood. Flower are a kind of gift that every girl love to have. Here are list of 10 flowers that can bring smile to your lady’s face.

1. IRIS…

Primary Meanings: Faith, Hope, Wisdom, Courage, Admiration Iris’s are so gorgeous, just being given one would make her day. Pick one up from the florists, and surprise her with it on the way home.

2. SUNFLOWER…

Primary Meanings: Adoration, Loyalty, Longevity There’s nothing like sunflowers to put a smile on your face. How about buying a pack of seeds and seeing who can#grow the tallest one?

3. WILD FLOWERS…

Primary Meanings: Whimsical, spontaneous, and thoughtful With so many colors and styles, wild flowers are stunning. Pick some to go with some Lilac’s or Orchid’s, and you’ll produce a gorgeous bouquet she’s sure to love

4. LILAC…

Primary Meanings: Youthful, Innocence

5. CARNATION…

Primary Meanings: Fascination, Distinction. Love Carnations are #beautiful, but also very easy to grow. Plant some outside your front door, and she’ll be pleasantly surprised when they begin to bloom.

6. ORCHID…

Primary Meanings: Love, beauty, luxury, strength

7. LILY…

Primary Meanings: Friendship, Devotion These are my #absolute favorite flowers. You don’t need many for a gorgeous bouquet, and they are so classy. Give them to her at dinner, or just before you go for a night out. She’ll be amazed.

8. TULIP…

Primary Meanings: Perfect love Perfect for spring days, Tulips make gorgeous ‘Just because’ bouquets. Pick a few different colors, and tie them with a big ribbon.

9. DAISY…

Primary Meanings: Innocence, Purity, Cheerfulness How about picking some from around your house, and putting them in a vase for her? She’s sure to appreciate your handmade #bouquet!

10. ROSE…

Primary Meanings: Love, Romance Buy her a red rose, and she’ll be smitten. Put it in her water glass, or present it to her while on a date…it doesn’t matter. Rose’s are so romantic, she’s sure to be impressed.
